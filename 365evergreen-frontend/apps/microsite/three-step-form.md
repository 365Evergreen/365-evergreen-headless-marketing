Here’s a clean, human‑friendly three‑step form that keeps the tone positive, avoids jargon, and uses a mix of input types so it feels easy and inviting rather than like an audit.

## **Three‑Step Digital Workplace Discovery Form**

### **Step 1 — Your Current Experience**

Focus: How their workplace feels today, in plain language.

1. **How would you describe your day‑to‑day digital experience at work?** *Long text*

2. **Which of these statements best reflects how your current tools support your work?** *Single choice*
   
   - They help me get things done smoothly
   
   - They work, but could be better
   
   - They often slow me down
   
   - I’m not sure

3. **What parts of your digital workplace do you enjoy using?** *Multi‑select checkboxes*
   
   - Communication tools
   
   - File sharing
   
   - Collaboration spaces
   
   - Mobile access
   
   - Automation or shortcuts
   
   - Other (text field)

4. **How easy is it for you to find the information you need?** *Slider (0–10)*
   
   - 0 = Very difficult
   
   - 10 = Very easy

5. **If you could change one thing about your digital workplace today, what would it be?** *Short text*

### **Step 2 — What’s Working and What’s Not**

Focus: Strengths, frustrations, and opportunities.

1. **Which areas feel the most helpful or productive for you?** *Multi‑select checkboxes*
   
   - Communication
   
   - Team collaboration
   
   - Document management
   
   - Processes and workflows
   
   - Reporting and insights
   
   - Support and training

2. **Which areas feel the most challenging?** *Multi‑select checkboxes (same list as above)*

3. **How confident do you feel using your current tools?** *Single choice*
   
   - Very confident
   
   - Mostly confident
   
   - Sometimes unsure
   
   - Often unsure

4. **What slows you down the most when trying to get work done?** *Dropdown*
   
   - Hard to find information
   
   - Too many steps to complete tasks
   
   - Tools don’t work well together
   
   - Lack of guidance or training
   
   - Other (text field)

5. **Tell us about a recent moment where your digital tools helped you succeed.** *Long text* 
   (Keeps the tone positive and future‑focused)

### **Step 3 — Your Ideal Digital Workplace**

Focus: Aspirations, outcomes, and what “better” looks like.

1. **What would make your digital workplace feel more effortless?** *Long text*

2. **Which improvements would make the biggest difference for you?** *Multi‑select checkboxes*
   
   - Clearer communication
   
   - Easier access to information
   
   - Better collaboration spaces
   
   - More automation
   
   - Stronger support and guidance
   
   - A more consistent experience across tools

3. **How important is it for your digital workplace to improve in the next 12 months?** *Slider (0–10)*
   
   - 0 = Not important
   
   - 10 = Extremely important

4. **What outcomes would you love to see from a more modern workplace?** *Multi‑select checkboxes*
   
   - Saving time
   
   - Reducing frustration
   
   - Improving teamwork
   
   - Making processes simpler
   
   - Helping people feel more connected
   
   - Supporting growth and innovation

5. **If everything worked exactly the way you wanted, what would your ideal workday look like?** *Long text*
